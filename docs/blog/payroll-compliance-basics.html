Payroll Compliance Basics for Small Teams

Classify workers correctly

Review each role to determine employee vs contractor status and document your decision.

Stay on top of filings

File payroll taxes on time and keep a calendar for quarterly and annual deadlines.

  • Federal and state payroll tax deposits
  • Quarterly reporting and annual forms
  • Local compliance requirements as needed

Keep clean records

Maintain paystubs, time tracking, and benefit documentation for every pay period.
