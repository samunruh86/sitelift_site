Common Bookkeeping Mistakes (and How to Fix Them)

Stop skipping reconciliations

Reconcile accounts monthly so errors are caught early and reports stay trustworthy.

Avoid miscoding expenses

Use a consistent chart of accounts and keep a short list of rules for recurring transactions.

  • Match vendor names to the same category each time
  • Separate personal and business expenses
  • Document unusual transactions in a notes log

Review your reports with context

Compare this month to the prior month and to last year before making decisions.
