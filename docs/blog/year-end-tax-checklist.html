Year-End Tax Checklist for Business Owners

Close the year with clean books

Reconcile every account, review outstanding invoices, and confirm payroll totals before year-end.

Gather the right documents

Prepare a folder with statements, receipts, and tax forms so filing is faster and more accurate.

  • Bank and credit card statements
  • 1099s, payroll summaries, and contractor lists
  • Asset purchases and depreciation schedules

Review deductions and timing

Confirm eligible deductions and decide whether to accelerate expenses or defer income.

“A tidy checklist today saves hours of corrections later.”
